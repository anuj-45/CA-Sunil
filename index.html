Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services

What services do you provide?

+

We provide comprehensive CA services including tax planning and filing, auditing, GST services, business consulting, financial planning, and company formation assistance. Our team specializes in serving both individuals and businesses across various industries.

How much do your services cost?

+

Our pricing varies based on the scope and complexity of services required. We offer customized packages tailored to your specific needs. Contact us for a free consultation and detailed quote.

Do you handle GST filing and compliance?

+

Yes, we provide complete GST solutions including registration, monthly/quarterly return filing, GST audit, and compliance advisory. We ensure timely filing and help you stay compliant with all GST regulations.

Can you help with income tax return filing?

+

Absolutely! We handle income tax return filing for individuals, businesses, and corporations. We also provide tax planning strategies to help minimize your tax liability legally and efficiently.

How long does company registration take?

+

Company registration typically takes 7-15 business days, depending on the type of company and approval processes. We handle all documentation and liaising with authorities to ensure a smooth registration process.

Do you offer consultation services?

+

Yes, we offer business and financial consultation services. Our first consultation is free, where we understand your requirements and suggest the best solutions for your financial goals.
